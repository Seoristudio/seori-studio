About

Lee Seori is an AI visual artist
based in Korea, working under
the name Seori Studio.

Her ongoing project, Confetti Life, reimagines ordinary scenes through pointillist texture, glittering surfaces, and warm color.

Rather than building fantasy worlds far from reality, her work begins with familiar things — a city corner, a piece of fruit, a cafe table, a quiet walk — and lets them slip just slightly out of the real.

Open to collaborations and brand projects.
